Issue 4.1 / 9.1

Arbitrary payment caps (25k / 71.5k / 30k / 15k / 10k)

BeforeallocatePaymentToLedger() auto-split payments across 7 slots using hardcoded caps (fi=25k, v2=71.5k, altV2=71.5k, v3=71.5k, t1=30k, t2=15k, t3=10k).
