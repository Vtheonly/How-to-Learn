# 2.4. Engineering Stress-Tolerant Execution

When a crisis strikes, your brain's sympathetic nervous system activates, releasing a surge of adrenaline and cortisol that can easily shut down your prefrontal cortex. To maintain high-level cognitive execution under pressure, you must build an **Autonomic Gating Protocol (AGP)** to manually control your body's stress response.

    Trigger[Crisis / Sudden Stressor] --> Sens_Detect[1. Autonomic Sensing: Detect physical stress signatures]
    Sens_Detect --> Manual_Override[2. Autonomic Override: Manually lower heart rate via breathing]
    Manual_Override --> Cognitive_Gating[3. Cognitive Gating: Execute strict diagnostic checklists]
    Cognitive_Gating --> Execution[4. Controlled Execution: System 2 remains online]
