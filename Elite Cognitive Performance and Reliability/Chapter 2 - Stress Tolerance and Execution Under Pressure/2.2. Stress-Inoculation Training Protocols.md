# 2.2. Stress-Inoculation Training Protocols

## 1. What is Stress-Inoculation Training (SIT)?

Developed by psychologist Donald Meichenbaum, **Stress-Inoculation Training (SIT)** is a cognitive-behavioral intervention that functions like a physical vaccine. By exposing yourself to controlled, manageable levels of stress, you build up your psychological immunity and prevent cognitive overload during real-world crises.
