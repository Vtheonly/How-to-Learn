# 6.2. Dopamine Nation (Dr. Anna Lembke)

## 1. Core Thesis
Dr. Anna Lembke, Chief of the Stanford Addiction Medicine Dual Diagnosis Clinic, explores the neurobiology of pleasure, pain, and addiction in our highly digital, instant-gratification world. She demonstrates how flooding our brains with high-dopamine stimuli (such as social media, gaming, and online pornography) degrades our baseline focus, drive, and mental resilience.
