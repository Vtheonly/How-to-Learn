## 1. John Ousterhout’s Complexity Formula

In *A Philosophy of Software Design*, John Ousterhout defines the overall complexity of a system ($C$) mathematically:

$$C = \sum_{p} c_p \cdot t_p$$

* $c_p$ represents the complexity of a specific part $p$ of the codebase.
* $t_p$ represents the fraction of time developers spend working on or maintaining that specific part.
