# 1.4. The Cognitive Compilation Pipeline

To achieve on-demand competence, you must stop treating learning as an act of passive absorption. You must treat your brain as a compiler that translates raw information into executable cognitive code.

```mermaid
graph TD
    RawData[Raw Information: Unstructured, noisy, high entropy] --> Parse[1. Parser Phase: Deconstruct into semantic primitives]
    Parse --> Map[2. Semantic Mapping: Bind primitives to existing mental schemas]
    Map --> Optimize[3. Optimization Phase: Spaced active recall and error testing]
    Optimize --> Execute[4. Runtime Execution: Shift from PFC to Basal Ganglia]
