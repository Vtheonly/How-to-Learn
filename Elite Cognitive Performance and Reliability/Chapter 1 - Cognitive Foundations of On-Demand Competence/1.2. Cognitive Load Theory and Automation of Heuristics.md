# 1.2. Cognitive Load Theory and Automation of Heuristics

## 1. Theoretical Foundations: Cognitive Load Theory (CLT)

John Sweller’s Cognitive Load Theory (CLT) is based on a fundamental premise of evolutionary psychology: **human working memory capacity (WMC) is extremely limited.**
