# 3.1. Designing High-Reliability Execution Frameworks

## 1. What are High-Reliability Organizations (HROs)?

**High-Reliability Organizations (HROs)** operate in complex, hazardous environments for long periods without making catastrophic errors (e.g., nuclear power plants, aircraft carriers, air traffic control centers).
