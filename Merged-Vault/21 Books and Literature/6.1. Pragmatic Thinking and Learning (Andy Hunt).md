# 6.1. Pragmatic Thinking and Learning (Andy Hunt)

## 1. Core Thesis
Co-founder of the Pragmatic Bookshelf Andy Hunt applies cognitive science, systems thinking, and behavioral psychology specifically to the work and lives of software developers. He treats the developer's brain as "wetware" that can be refactored, optimized, and debugged just like software.
