# 6.3. A Philosophy of Software Design (John Ousterhout)

## 1. Core Thesis
Stanford Professor John Ousterhout presents a highly systematic approach to software engineering, arguing that **the primary goal of software design is to manage and minimize cognitive load.** He defines complexity as anything that makes a software system difficult to understand, modify, or maintain.
