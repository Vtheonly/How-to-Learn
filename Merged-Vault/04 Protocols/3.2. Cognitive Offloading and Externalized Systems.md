# 3.2. Cognitive Offloading and Externalized Systems

## 1. Theoretical Foundations: The Extended Mind Thesis

Proposed by philosophers Andy Clark and David Chalmers, the **Extended Mind Thesis** argues that the human mind does not stop at the skull.
