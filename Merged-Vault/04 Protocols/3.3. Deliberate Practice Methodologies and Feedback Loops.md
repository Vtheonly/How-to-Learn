# 3.3. Deliberate Practice Methodologies and Feedback Loops

## 1. Theoretical Foundations: Deliberate Practice

Based on the research of psychologist K. Anders Ericsson, **Deliberate Practice** is a highly structured form of training designed specifically to improve performance. It is the exact opposite of mindlessly repeating things you are already good at.

    DP[Deliberate Practice Core Elements] --> E1[Specific Goals: Target single, well-defined parts of a skill]
    DP --> E2[High Focus: Demands your full concentration, with zero distractions]
    DP --> E3[Immediate Feedback: Instant, clear information on your performance]
    DP --> E4[Stepping Out of Comfort: Consistently working on things that are difficult]
