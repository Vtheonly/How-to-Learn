# 5.2. Cognitive Re-framing of Self-Sabotage and Imposter Syndrome

## 1. The Mechanics of Self-Sabotage: The Alibi Heuristic

Self-sabotage is not a random act of stupidity; it is a subconscious **ego-defense mechanism** designed to protect your self-esteem from the risk of failure.

```mermaid
graph TD
    HighStakes[High-Stakes Challenge / Project Deadline] --> Vulnerability[Fear of Failure: "If I try my best and fail, it proves I am stupid"]
    Vulnerability --> SelfSabotage[Self-Sabotage: Procrastinating, staying up late, avoiding work]
    
    SelfSabotage --> Outcome_Fail[Outcome: Performance Fails]
    Outcome_Fail --> Alibi[The Alibi: "I only failed because I didn't start until the last minute. I'm actually smart."]
    
    SelfSabotage --> Outcome_Pass[Outcome: Performance Succeeds]
    Outcome_Pass --> Genius[The Genius Ego: "I got an A and I only started study at 3 AM. I am a genius."]
